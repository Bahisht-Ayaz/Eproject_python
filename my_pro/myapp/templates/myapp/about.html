The Climate Group

To join a climate change team, you can volunteer with environmental organizations, join government or NGO pacts and networks, or create a climate-focused role within your existing workplace. Options range from contributing to large-scale initiatives like the Race to Zero campaign and the European Climate Pact, to joining a specific organization like Earth.Org or the Pakistan Environment Trust, or participating in smaller peer networks.

Join the Race to Zero: This campaign encourages cities, regions, and businesses to commit to net-zero emissions and requires members to develop decarbonization pathways and disclose their progress.
